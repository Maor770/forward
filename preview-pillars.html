Project #1

Maor Headquarters & Visitor Center

A multi-floor building housing production offices, distribution center, and an interactive Visitor Center.

  • Office Space. Physical space required to plan, create, and distribute our three new program tracks and distribute Maor products.

  • Visitor Center. Kids will be able to experience Maor's mission live! They will step into a 3D, live-action scenario where they face real-life challenges.

    They will use the Rebbe's sichos to navigate these scenarios, walking out empowered and thinking, "Wow, I can do this in my real life."

  • Rentals. Generating rental income to sustain the building.
Project #2

Custom, Proprietary AI System

Current AI models default to secular concepts and humor. The custom system we are building allows us strict control over the narrative, characters, and messaging, ensuring every frame aligns with the Rebbe's teachings.

  • Script Creation. A custom-built AI generation system specifically trained on the Rebbe's values and visual guidelines.
  • Animations. We will produce Disney-quality, 3D animations for just $2,000–$3,000 per video (a fraction of the industry standard $80k+).
  • New Channels. We will create custom AI systems to create videos for the non-Lubavitch, not-frum and non-Jewish child, targeting them and furthering the mission.
